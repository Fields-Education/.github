# Fields Education Inc.

Real projects, real people, real possibilities.

Fields Education Inc. builds language learning and cross-cultural experiences that help students do something real with what they learn. We combine classroom technology, exchange programs, trips, events, and other immersive education experiences to help learners build confidence through real-world projects, meaningful connection, and global perspective.

## What We Build

- Language learning experiences built around real-world use
- An integrated set of platforms for classroom learning, communication, exchange, and program management
- Programs and community experiences that connect students across schools, cultures, and borders
- Event organization and program management for trips, camps, and other experiences
- Fields Marketplace for discovering, organizing, and managing trips, camps, and events
- Recognition pathways that help learners demonstrate what they can do

## Products

- [joinfields.org](https://joinfields.org) - general marketing site for Fields Education Inc.
- [fields.app](https://fields.app) - Fields Classroom, the classroom product for learning, collaboration, and real-world projects
- [fields.org](https://fields.org) - Fields Marketplace, the platform for discovering, organizing, and managing trips, camps, and events

## Our Approach

- Learn by doing
- Real projects with real outcomes
- Language learning through real communication
- Cross-cultural exchange and shared experiences
- Trips, camps, events, and other immersive programs
- Tools that support the real logistics behind those experiences
- Education that opens real-world possibilities
